NEXT CONCERT

Holiday Candlelight

November 21, 2021 at 7:30pm
At the Merced Theatre
Doors open at 6:30pm

Join the Merced Symphony in our traditional and beloved Holiday Candlelight! Nothing gets one into the holiday mood like some timeless classics played by a symphony chamber orchestra. Be serenaded as we perform concert favorites that will get you in the holiday mood. Lyric Coloratura soprano Nikki Einfeld will be our soloist for this evening of holiday classics. Ms. Einfeld is a former Adler Fellow and Merola Alumnus with the San Francisco Opera and has performed across North America with some of the most notable orchestras.

Upcoming Concert

From Film to Stage!

January 21, 2022 at 7:30pm
At the Merced Theatre
Doors open at 6:30pm

Some of the greatest orchestral music has been found in movie theaters as composers like John Williams, Bernard Hermann, and Hans Zimmer all were taught and influenced by classical composers. Join the Merced Symphony in a classic pops concert that will thrill and excite you in the theater – with some of the most famous movie music written for the screen!

Upcoming Concert

Season Finale

May 21, 2022 at 7:30pm
At Central Presbyterian Church
Doors open at 6:30pm

We close out the 64th Anniversary season with two momentous works, Camille Saint-Saëns' grand Organ Symphony and the Brahms Double Concerto for Violin and Cello. Hear this immense, grand and sweeping symphony of Saint-Saëns as we close out the season with one of the fullest orchestras on stage. Soloists Cordula Merks and Amos Yang come from the San Francisco Symphony and San Francisco Ballet and perform the fiendishly difficult but beautiful Brahms Double Concerto. An exciting way to finish out the year, this will be a concert, and a season, not to miss!
