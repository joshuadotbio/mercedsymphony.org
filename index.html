Free Broadcast Concert “Back On Stage!”

Re-connect with your Merced Symphony on Friday, Sept. 11, 2020 at 7 p.m. as the Merced Theatre comes alive for a performance of Vivaldi’s Four Seasons, Tchaikovsky’s Serenade for Strings, and other classics spiced with an electric violin under the direction of Maestro Ming Luke.

Access “Back On Stage” on Comcast channel 97, AT&T U-verse channel 99, MCOE’s Facebook and YouTube accounts, and at www.mcoe.org/metv.

This live-streamed production is made possible by the Merced County Office of Education in collaboration with the Merced Theatre Foundation.
