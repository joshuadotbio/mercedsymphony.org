Season Finale “Reconnecting” In-Person Concert Saturday, July 3, 2021 @ 7:30 PM

Watch on Youtube

The Merced Symphony triumphantly returns live, in-person symphonic music to the Merced Theatre! Featuring Felix Mendelssohn’s Reformation, Antonin Dvorak’s New World, and An American In Paris by George Gershwin, this performance joins in Merced’s celebration of America with powerful melodies and familiar tunes that are not to be missed!

Tickets are $25 for general admission or $10 for students with ID. Purchase at tickets.mercedtheatre.org or at the Merced Theatre Box Office.

Live-stream available on MSA’s YouTube channel. (Note: An American In Paris will not be broadcast.)
