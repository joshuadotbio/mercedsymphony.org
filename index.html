Upcoming Concert

Holiday Candlelight

Join the Merced Symphony in our traditional and beloved Holiday Candlelight! Nothing gets one into the holiday mood like some timeless classics played by a symphony chamber orchestra. Be serenaded as we perform concert favorites that will get you in the holiday mood. Lyric Coloratura soprano Nikki Einfeld will be our soloist for this evening of holiday classics. Ms. Einfeld is a former Adler Fellow and Merola Alumnus with the San Francisco Opera and has performed across North America with some of the most notable orchestras.
