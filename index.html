All-New “Holiday Candlelight” Concert Streaming Now!

Watch on Youtube

Back by popular demand! Your Merced Symphony brings our annual Holiday Candlelight concert to the comfort and safety of your own home! Classic holiday pieces include O Holy Night and We Wish You a Merry Christmas played by our String Quartet; Silent Night and Ceremonial For Christmas Brass played by our Brass Quintet; and Arcangelo Corelli’s Christmas, Concerto grosso in G minor, Op. 6, No. 8 played by our String Ensemble. Enjoy this special performance by many of your favorite Merced Symphony players under the direction of Maestro Ming Luke.

This pre-recorded production was produced in collaboration with First Presbyterian Church of Oakland, Director of Music and Organist Aaron Nee, and Interim Facility Manager Christopher Weber. Funding was made possible by our generous patrons and federal Cares Act funds. Please consider a donation to the Merced Symphony to help keep live symphonic music alive in Downtown Merced!
